One Piece Characters

1. Monkey D. Luffy:

-Monkey D. Luffy
-the most popular character of all time in One Piece. He is our adored main character,
and right now he is enjoying the time of his life while getting OP. Luffy set off on his quest to
become the next pirate king, and he is headed in the right direction. Luffy has gone a
long way and continues to be an inspiration today. He started out as a young child who stretched out his arms
to beat enemies to become an emperor of the sea.

2.Roronoa Zoro:

-Roronoa Zoro

-is the swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates. Prior to becoming a pirate, he was a bounty hunter
known as Pirate Hunter Zoro. He transitioned from pursuing pirates for a reward to becoming
one himself. Can you actually believe that? The Straw Hats wouldnt be the same without Zoro, though.
Zoro made the proper choice to join the crew of the future pirate king since his goal is to defeat
Dracule Mihawk and become the greatest swordsman in the world.
Since Zoro was willing to risk his life in Thriller Bark to save his captain Luffy, his loyalty to him cannot be questioned.
From that point on, Zoro went on to become a favorite of all One Piece fans. Zoro is the right-hand man
of Luffy and is currently the second most popular character in One Piece as a result.

3.Shanks:

-Shanks
It would be foolish to leave Shanks off the list of the most popular One Piece characters. He is the
, the myth, and the legend. Shanks is a popular character, thanks to his fierce attitude despite
getting minimal screen time but only occasionally appearing. He became one of the sea's
emperors and did not eat devil fruit. Though fans frequently argue about whether he is a hero or will soon
become a villain, his legacy will always be discussed aloud.
Our beloved Luffy was inspired by Shanks to begin his pirate escapades. Released in 2022, One Piece Film: Red has been a terrific
treat for Shanks fans. As manga readers, we can affirm that a huge treat is coming to come for all
of the fans of the anime. Shanks continues to be in third place as a result.

4.Gol D. Roger:

-Gol D. Roger.
This list wouldn't be complete without the Pirate King Gol D. Roger. Roger is an essential addition
to our One Piece key characters list. The great pirate age was inaugurated by him when he gave a
rousing speech as he was about to leave this world. With the discovery of the fabled One Piece,
Gol D Roger became the first Pirate King. Without using any devil fruit abilities, he accomplished
all of this while dominating the seas. Just imagine what a strong character he is!
The pirate generation we are currently seeing in One Piece is highly unlikely to have existed without Roger.
Having all that, Roger is Roger, and he is unquestionably one of the most popular characters in One Piece.

5.Vinsmoke Sanji:

-Vinsmoke Sanji
The Straw Heart Pirates chef, Sanji, is also the second-in-command to Zoro. Along with Zoro and Luffy,
he is a member of the monster trio. Sanji is a versatile character as he excels in combat
with his scorching, ferocious strikes and simultaneously prepares exquisite meals for the entire crew.
Sanji has a sizable following base, thanks to his regard for his attitude and respect toward women.
Luffy mentioned that he cannot become the pirate king without Sanji. So yeah,
that's how much Luffy loves him and holds him important to the crew. Thus, Sanji remains fifth on our
list of popular characters in One Piece.

6.Nico Robin:

-Nico Robin

-Nico Robin
Nico Robin is the Straw Hat Pirate's archaeologist. She was introduced as one of the adversaries and unexpectedly
joined the Straw Hats at the end of the Alabasta arc. As frightening as she appeared at first,
she is the absolute opposite of that. Robin-chwan is a kind and down-to-earth person.
Her life was filled with tragedy, but with the Straw Hats, she wants to help her friends and fulfill her
ambition of discovering the world's history.Robin has proven her usefulness in
fights and otherwise, thanks to her vast understanding of history and poneglyphs. Robin is the strongest female member of Straw Hats
and is appreciated by people all around the world.
As a result, Nico Robin remains the tenth most popular character in One Piece.

7.White Beard:

-White Beard
Edward Newgate Aka Whitebeard was called the strongest man in the world. He was the direct rival to our Pirate King
Roger and that speaks of how mighty he was. When we were first introduced to him, he seemed
like a fearless old man. But the flashback scene of him was more than enough to show the
true colors of this man. Whilst most of the pirates set out to the seas to find treasure, all he ever
wanted was a family.Even when he was not in his prime, Whitebeard shook the world
with his bravest acts in the Marineford war for Ace. Whitebeard is one of the most down-to-earth characters in the show and
is respectfully called Oyaji like how his crewmates call him by the fans.
Whitebeard ranks at 7th position in the list of most popular characters in One Piece

8.Nami:

-Nami
Nami is the navigator in the Straw Hat Pirates crew. She was earlier known as Cat Burglar Nami because she used to rob pirates.
But after joining the Straw Hat Pirates, she began to win over fans hearts.
Nami is a loving and affectionate woman who strengthens the Straw Hats’ friendship. She has excellent
navigation abilities and can assist in fights when necessary. The heart stealer Nami is one of the
founding members of the Straw Hats and is one of the first characters we were introduced to in the OP anime.
Since then, she has remained in our hearts forever. That's why she remains in the 8th position in the list of most popular characters in One Piece.
